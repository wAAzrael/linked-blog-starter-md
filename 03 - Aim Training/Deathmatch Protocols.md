# Deathmatch Protocols

## Protocol 1 — First-Bullet Accuracy

- Use Vandal
- Prioritize headshots
- Do not crouch spray
- Reset after every burst
- Ignore the scoreboard

## Protocol 2 — Mid-Range Movement

- Burst
- Strafe
- Burst again
- Reposition after kills
- Avoid standing still for long exchanges

## Protocol 3 — Long-Range Control

- Seek long sightlines
- Tap or use 2–3 bullet bursts
- Confirm the stop before shooting
- Do not force close-range farming

## Evaluation

After each DM, score:
- Movement: /10
- Shot discipline: /10
- Crosshair placement: /10
- Tension level: /10

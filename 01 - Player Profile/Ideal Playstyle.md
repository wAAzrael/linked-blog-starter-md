# Ideal Playstyle

## Description

A mechanically sharp controller who:
- Survives long enough to use multiple utility cycles
- Can take high-value fights
- Creates pressure without feeding
- Communicates a clear plan
- Understands when to support, lurk, regroup, or lead
- Remains composed in chaotic rounds

## Avoid

- Passive utility dumping
- Solo hero plays without information
- Taking aim duels that remove late-round utility
- Playing too safely when the team needs initiative

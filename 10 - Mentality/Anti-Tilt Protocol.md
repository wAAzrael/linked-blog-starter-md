# Anti-Tilt Protocol

## Warning Signs

- Blaming teammates every round
- Queueing to recover RR
- Tensing the mouse hand
- Taking revenge peeks
- Ignoring the plan
- Muting useful information
- Feeling rushed between games

## Immediate Reset

1. Leave the queue
2. Stand up
3. Drink water
4. Review one controllable mistake
5. Decide whether the next game will be high quality

## Stop Conditions

Stop ranked when:
- Two games in a row are low quality
- You are no longer communicating
- You are repeatedly ignoring your focus
- You are playing only to recover RR

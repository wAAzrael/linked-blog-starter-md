# Crosshair Placement

## Principles

- Pre-aim likely head positions
- Adjust for elevation changes
- Keep the crosshair close to the angle
- Avoid staring at walls while moving
- Change placement based on enemy weapon and likely peek

## Review Questions

- Was my crosshair ready before the enemy appeared?
- Was I aiming too close or too far from the corner?
- Did I account for wide peeks?

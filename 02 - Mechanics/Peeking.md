# Peeking

## Types

- Jiggle peek
- Shoulder bait
- Slice-the-pie clear
- Wide swing
- Contact peek
- Swing from teammate utility
- Repeek

## Decision Rule

Do not ask only “Can I win this duel?”

Ask:
- What information do I gain?
- Can I be traded?
- Can I escape?
- Does the team still need my utility?

# Recurring Mistakes

## Active Mistakes

### 1. Shooting before fully stopping
- **Category:** Mechanics
- **Trigger:** Medium- or long-range duel
- **Correction:** Confirm movement stop before firing
- **Status:** Active

### 2. Unnecessary first deaths as controller
- **Category:** Decision-making
- **Trigger:** Taking unsupported early fights
- **Correction:** Ask whether the team still needs my utility
- **Status:** Active

### 3. Autopilot during long sessions
- **Category:** Mentality
- **Trigger:** Instant queueing and repeated impulsive plays
- **Correction:** Use the stop-loss and session reset
- **Status:** Active

## Resolved Mistakes

Move mistakes here only after repeated evidence of improvement.

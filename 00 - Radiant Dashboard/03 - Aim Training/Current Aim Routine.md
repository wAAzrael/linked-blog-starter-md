w# Current Aim Routine

## Pre-Ranked Routine

### 1. Warm-up — 5 minutes
- Easy smooth tracking
- Relaxed grip
- Full range of motion

### 2. Precision — 10 minutes
- Small static targets
- Controlled micro-corrections
- Accuracy over speed

### 3. Dynamic Clicking — 10 minutes
- Target reading
- Clean first click
- Avoid rushed flicks

### 4. Valorant Range — 5 minutes
- Bots with movement
- Bursts and taps
- Counter-strafe timing

### 5. Focused Deathmatch — 1 or 2 games
- First-bullet accuracy
- Mid- and long-range fights
- No score chasing

## Stop Rule

Do not turn the warm-up into a full training session before ranked.

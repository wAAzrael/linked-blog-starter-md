# Radiant Mentality

## Principles

- Play the situation, not the rank icon
- Confidence comes from preparation and evidence
- A mistake is information, not identity
- Avoid emotional queueing
- Focus on repeatable decisions
- Stay curious during losses
- Do not confuse intensity with focus

## Identity

> I do not need to prove I belong in the lobby. I need to make the best available decision, one round at a time.

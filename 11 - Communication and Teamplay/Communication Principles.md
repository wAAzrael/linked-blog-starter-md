# Communication Principles

## Good Communication Is

- Early
- Clear
- Short
- Actionable
- Calm
- Specific

## Controller Examples

- “Smoking Heaven and Tree now.”
- “Wait two seconds for my smoke.”
- “I can paranoia through Main.”
- “I have one smoke in ten.”
- “Do not rotate yet; they have not committed.”
- “I can support your swing.”

## Avoid

- Narrating everything
- Complaining mid-round
- Vague calls
- Calling plans without checking teammate positions

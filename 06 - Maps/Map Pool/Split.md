# Split

> Current focus: **Layer utility before Mid fights. Dry Mid deaths ruin Split halves.**

## Map Identity

Compressed vertical map. Mid/Ropes control decides both sites, and utility layering is stronger than raw dry peeks.

### Main Win Conditions

- Win Mid/Ropes pressure or convincingly fake it.
- Use smokes to cross tight chokes.
- On defense, deny Mid without dying alone.


### Important Areas

- A Main
- A Ramps
- Ropes
- Mid Mail/Vents
- B Main
- B Heaven


### Rotation Characteristics

- Defender rotations are fast through Heaven/Ropes. Attackers need Mid control to stop stacked sites.

### Common Defender Setups

- Common setups: one A anchor, one Ramps, one Mid, one B Heaven, one B anchor/rotator.

### Common Attacker Default

- Pressure Mid with two, hold B Main/A Main pushes, then split A or B through Heaven/Ropes.

### Best Controller From My Pool

- Omen and Astra are excellent. Clove is strong for ranked if you fight after smoking. Reyna thrives in tight Leer fights.

## Attack

### Default Responsibilities

| Phase | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Early round | Take safe space, deny info, keep spike protected, avoid first death. |
| Mid-round | Read utility/rotations, call regroup or split, preserve one smoke if possible. |
| Late round | Smoke final choke, trade plant, set post-plant crossfire. |

### Default Plan

- Mid control is priority; do not dry walk into Mail/Vents crossfire.
- Use smoke on Mail/Vents to isolate one fight.
- Hold defensive pushes from A/B Main while Mid group works.
- If Mid fails twice, fake Mid and fast hit an extremity.

### A Execute

- Take A Main + Ramps if possible.
- Smoke Heaven/Rafters and Screens/CT depending on route.
- Clear close site, Hell, Rafters drop.
- Plant safe if Heaven not controlled; plant for Main if space is won.
- Post-plant: one Main, one site, one Heaven/Ramps if possible.

### B Execute

- Take B Main with utility; clear close, default, back site.
- Smoke Heaven and CT/Alley.
- Best version is B split from Mid/Vents + B Main.
- Post-plant: avoid all B Main; keep one site/backsite if safe.

### Mid-Round Rules

- If defenders smoke Mid early, wait and re-hit with second wave.
- If they fight Mid, punish with stun/flash/Leer and trade.
- If they fully give Mid, split every round until they fight.


### Anti-Eco Rules

- Do not walk alone into close shotgun corners.
- Use one smoke to cross dangerous long angle, then clear close with numbers.
- Keep spike away from the first-contact player.
- If you get first pick, slow down and trade; do not donate weapons.

### Bonus Round Rules

- Avoid dry long-range fights versus rifles.
- Use numbers and utility to isolate one defender.
- If spike is planted, bonus is already successful; play post-plant trades.

## Defense

### Setup 1 - Standard Information Setup

- Controller plays Mid/Ropes support. Smoke Mid choke after contact, not instantly every round. Chamber can hold B Main or A Ramp angle.

### Setup 2 - Adapt / Retake Setup

- Aggressive Ramps/Mid round with Reyna + controller support, then fall after one kill.

### Rotation Rules

- Rotate on **spike seen**, **multiple enemies confirmed**, or **key utility committed**, not on one footstep.
- If you are the anchor, your first job is to stay alive and delay.
- If teammate dies alone, do not instantly replace the same angle without utility.
- Call what you are giving up: `I am leaving B Main`, `I am holding Tree`, `I have no smoke for retake`.

### Anchor Rules

- One kill and survival is better than two kills and no delay if the site collapses.
- Use smoke to split the execute wave.
- Fight when attackers cross utility, plant, or turn from teammate contact.

## Retakes

- A retake: smoke Main or Screens, clear Hell, swing Rafters/site together.
- B retake: smoke B Main, clear close/default/backsite, avoid single-file Heaven drops.
- Save paranoia/decay/pull for clearing tight pockets.


## Pistol Rounds

### Attack Pistol

- B split or A Ramps split. Tight Leer/Classic armor rounds are strong.

### Defense Pistol

- Stack Mid/Ropes or B Heaven. Chamber Headhunter can lock a choke.

## Agent Plans

### Omen

- Premium pick. Smoke Mail/Vents, Heaven, CT.
- Paranoia through Mid/Ropes/A Main wins compressed fights.
- TP to boxes/one-and-done angles after smokes.

### Astra

- Excellent for Mid control. Pull Mail/Vents or B default.
- Stun Ropes/Rafters for site hits.
- Recall Mid star to fake pressure and split late.

### Clove

- Strong for taking Mid fights after Ruse.
- Meddle tight corners before Reyna/you swing.
- Ult has high value because sites are close and trades happen fast.

### Reyna

- Very strong on Split: Leer through tight chokes and Dismiss out.
- Fight Mid/Ramps with support, not solo.
- Avoid overheal greed after first kill.

### Chamber

- Hold B Main/A Ramp or Mid angle with Rendezvous.
- Trademark flank or Ropes depending on team.
- Headhunter strong in pistol/eco tight angles.


## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Loses | Correction |
|---|---|---|
| Smoking instantly every round | Gives defenders timing and lets them wait it out. | Smoke with pressure or for a specific crossing. |
| Dying before second layer utility | Team loses execute/retake power. | Play one step behind first contact unless on Reyna/Chamber plan. |
| Rotating on sound only | Leaves site open to fakes. | Ask for spike, contact count, or utility confirmation. |
| Playing post-plant from one lane | Easy retake collapse. | Create crossfire: main + site + flank/late lane. |
| Taking ego duel after advantage | Throws numbers. | After pick, group and force trades. |

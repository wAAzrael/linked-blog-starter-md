# Breeze

> Current focus: **Long-range calm. Cross open space only with smoke, flash, or teammate trade.**

## Map Identity

Long-range, space-heavy map. Poor spacing and panic rotates lose more rounds than bad aim.

### Main Win Conditions

- Control long lanes with patience, not random wide peeks.
- Use late smokes to cross open space.
- Keep flank control because rotations are long and lurks are round-winning.


### Important Areas

- A Main / Cave
- Mid Pillar / Mid Nest
- B Main
- Elbow
- Tunnel
- Halls


### Rotation Characteristics

- Long rotations. Once spike is seen, defenders often over-rotate slowly; attackers can punish with late lurks.

### Common Defender Setups

- Common setups: anchors deep, one Mid info player, one Halls/rotate player.

### Common Attacker Default

- Spread map, deny OP info, take Mid/Halls slowly, then group for site hit.

### Best Controller From My Pool

- From your pool: Astra/Omen if team lacks Viper; Clove only with disciplined early survival. In pro-style comps Viper is often preferred, so compensate with better spacing and late smokes.

## Attack

### Default Responsibilities

| Phase | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Early round | Take safe space, deny info, keep spike protected, avoid first death. |
| Mid-round | Read utility/rotations, call regroup or split, preserve one smoke if possible. |
| Late round | Smoke final choke, trade plant, set post-plant crossfire. |

### Default Plan

- Never dry cross long sightlines alone.
- Use smoke to cross, not just to block spawn.
- Hold for defender pushes because Breeze defenders often take Halls/Mid info.
- Re-hit after defenders use recon/drone/flash early.

### A Execute

- Take A Main/Cave space and clear close angles before committing.
- Smoke defender spawn/bridge-side sightline and one open cross if needed.
- Split from Mid/Halls when possible; single-lane A hits are easy to spam.
- Plant only after clearing close pyramids/default danger zones.
- Post-plant: play long-range crossfires, not five close site.

### B Execute

- Take B Main slowly; clear close, back site, elbow-side pressure.
- Smoke CT/Defender spawn lane and block elbow if team cannot clear it.
- Use Mid split through tunnel when defenders stack B Main.
- Plant for Main only if you have B Main control and numbers.
- Post-plant: one flank watch minimum.

### Mid-Round Rules

- Mid is not optional; it prevents defenders from freely stacking sites.
- If OP is posted Mid/Halls, use smoke to cross and deny re-peek.
- Avoid rotating late through loud, exposed lanes without a lurk.


### Anti-Eco Rules

- Do not walk alone into close shotgun corners.
- Use one smoke to cross dangerous long angle, then clear close with numbers.
- Keep spike away from the first-contact player.
- If you get first pick, slow down and trade; do not donate weapons.

### Bonus Round Rules

- Avoid dry long-range fights versus rifles.
- Use numbers and utility to isolate one defender.
- If spike is planted, bonus is already successful; play post-plant trades.

## Defense

### Setup 1 - Standard Information Setup

- Play deep, use early info utility from teammates, keep smoke for execute crossing. Chamber can hold long OP angle then Rendezvous out.

### Setup 2 - Adapt / Retake Setup

- Aggro Halls/Mid info round with Reyna/Chamber, but leave controller alive for late smokes.

### Rotation Rules

- Rotate on **spike seen**, **multiple enemies confirmed**, or **key utility committed**, not on one footstep.
- If you are the anchor, your first job is to stay alive and delay.
- If teammate dies alone, do not instantly replace the same angle without utility.
- Call what you are giving up: `I am leaving B Main`, `I am holding Tree`, `I have no smoke for retake`.

### Anchor Rules

- One kill and survival is better than two kills and no delay if the site collapses.
- Use smoke to split the execute wave.
- Fight when attackers cross utility, plant, or turn from teammate contact.

## Retakes

- Retake needs spawn smokes and patience; do not sprint through open lane one by one.
- Use controller smoke to isolate post-plant Main or back-site player.
- Chamber saves Operator if retake is mathematically bad.


## Pistol Rounds

### Attack Pistol

- Ghost value is high. Avoid five-man sprint. Take one lane, punish rotates, then commit.

### Defense Pistol

- Play range with Ghost/Headhunter. Do not overpush into Classic stacks.

## Agent Plans

### Omen

- Use long-range smokes to cross, not just standard site smokes.
- Paranoia is best through chokepoints after teammates close distance.
- TP off-angles can punish long fights, but escape path first.

### Astra

- Global stars help fake across huge map.
- Pull/stun common plant or back-site angles before execute.
- Save at least one star for post-plant or retake.

### Clove

- Playable if you stay alive for second smoke; do not entry before smoking.
- Meddle to soften close site/Elbow pockets.
- Pick-Me-Up lets you take controlled long-range duels after team contact.

### Reyna

- Use Leer to cross long lanes and punish isolated anchors.
- Do not dismiss forward into open space without smoke.
- Hold long off-angles where first bullet matters.

### Chamber

- Best flex on Breeze from this pool. OP/Headhunter long lanes are high value.
- Rendezvous must cover long retreat, not cute close teleport.
- Trademark one flank because map is huge.


## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Loses | Correction |
|---|---|---|
| Smoking instantly every round | Gives defenders timing and lets them wait it out. | Smoke with pressure or for a specific crossing. |
| Dying before second layer utility | Team loses execute/retake power. | Play one step behind first contact unless on Reyna/Chamber plan. |
| Rotating on sound only | Leaves site open to fakes. | Ask for spike, contact count, or utility confirmation. |
| Playing post-plant from one lane | Easy retake collapse. | Create crossfire: main + site + flank/late lane. |
| Taking ego duel after advantage | Throws numbers. | After pick, group and force trades. |

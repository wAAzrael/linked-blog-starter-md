# Sunset

> Current focus: **Do not autopilot B Main. Combine B pressure with Mid threat.**

## Map Identity

Mid-control map with heavy B Main importance. If you lose Mid for free, defenders/attackers gain fast split pressure.

### Main Win Conditions

- Control Mid Bottom/Top timings.
- Pressure B Main without feeding into spam.
- Use late lurks through Mid to punish rotations.


### Important Areas

- A Main
- A Elbow
- Mid Top / Mid Bottom
- Market
- B Main
- Boba


### Rotation Characteristics

- Mid opens both sites and makes rotations dangerous. B Main pressure forces major defender utility.

### Common Defender Setups

- Common setups: A anchor, Mid player, B Main contest/anchor, Market rotator, flex.

### Common Attacker Default

- Take B Main or Mid control, then decide B split, A split, or fake B into A.

### Best Controller From My Pool

- Omen/Astra strong. Clove works well if you take aggressive Mid/B fights after smoking.

## Attack

### Default Responsibilities

| Phase | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Early round | Take safe space, deny info, keep spike protected, avoid first death. |
| Mid-round | Read utility/rotations, call regroup or split, preserve one smoke if possible. |
| Late round | Smoke final choke, trade plant, set post-plant crossfire. |

### Default Plan

- Pressure B Main early but do not die to spam/stall.
- Mid control lets you split both sites; fight it with utility.
- If Killjoy/Cypher hard locks B, pull rotations and end A.
- Use smoke to block Market/CT when splitting B.

### A Execute

- Take A Main, clear close elbow/site angles.
- Smoke Heaven/CT style defender angles and Link if needed.
- A hits need speed after utility because rotations are fast.
- Plant safe unless Elbow/Link controlled.
- Post-plant: Main + site crossfire; watch late Mid flank.

### B Execute

- Take B Main with flash/Leer/recon if possible.
- Smoke Market and CT/Defender spawn lane.
- Split through Mid/Market when possible.
- Plant for Main only when Main is safe and spam is controlled.
- Post-plant: one Main, one site, one Market/lurk if numbers allow.

### Mid-Round Rules

- Mid is your pressure valve; when B Main is stalled, go Mid.
- If defenders overfight Mid, punish with Reyna/Chamber trade setup.
- If defenders give Mid, split B until they change.


### Anti-Eco Rules

- Do not walk alone into close shotgun corners.
- Use one smoke to cross dangerous long angle, then clear close with numbers.
- Keep spike away from the first-contact player.
- If you get first pick, slow down and trade; do not donate weapons.

### Bonus Round Rules

- Avoid dry long-range fights versus rifles.
- Use numbers and utility to isolate one defender.
- If spike is planted, bonus is already successful; play post-plant trades.

## Defense

### Setup 1 - Standard Information Setup

- Fight B Main with support or smoke it and play retake. Controller should keep second smoke for Market/retake.

### Setup 2 - Adapt / Retake Setup

- Mid information setup: Chamber/Reyna takes supported first contact, controller blocks follow-up.

### Rotation Rules

- Rotate on **spike seen**, **multiple enemies confirmed**, or **key utility committed**, not on one footstep.
- If you are the anchor, your first job is to stay alive and delay.
- If teammate dies alone, do not instantly replace the same angle without utility.
- Call what you are giving up: `I am leaving B Main`, `I am holding Tree`, `I have no smoke for retake`.

### Anchor Rules

- One kill and survival is better than two kills and no delay if the site collapses.
- Use smoke to split the execute wave.
- Fight when attackers cross utility, plant, or turn from teammate contact.

## Retakes

- A retake: smoke Main/Elbow depending on plant, clear site pockets first.
- B retake: smoke B Main, clear close/default/back site, use Market + CT pinch.
- Do not tap spike without clearing Main spam angle.


## Pistol Rounds

### Attack Pistol

- B Main pressure into B split or fast A if B utility is heavy.

### Defense Pistol

- Fight B Main with two or stack Mid info. Chamber Headhunter can punish B Main.

## Agent Plans

### Omen

- Smoke Market/CT for B, Heaven/Link for A, Mid for control.
- Paranoia B Main/Mid splits.
- Use fake smokes to pull B rotator then end A.

### Astra

- Stars B Main, Mid, Market, A choke.
- Pull default plant/retake routes.
- Recall B star to fake pressure and split Mid late.

### Clove

- Great ranked map for aggressive Mid/B fights after smokes.
- Meddle B Main close/default pockets.
- Ult to convert B entry trade or stall defuse.

### Reyna

- Leer B Main or Mid to take first contact.
- Strong if you coordinate with controller smoke instead of dry swinging.
- Lurk Mid only when team creates B/A noise.

### Chamber

- Hold B Main/Mid angle; Rendezvous out after shot.
- Trademark flank or Mid lurk timing.
- Good eco value with Headhunter on B Main fights.


## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Loses | Correction |
|---|---|---|
| Smoking instantly every round | Gives defenders timing and lets them wait it out. | Smoke with pressure or for a specific crossing. |
| Dying before second layer utility | Team loses execute/retake power. | Play one step behind first contact unless on Reyna/Chamber plan. |
| Rotating on sound only | Leaves site open to fakes. | Ask for spike, contact count, or utility confirmation. |
| Playing post-plant from one lane | Easy retake collapse. | Create crossfire: main + site + flank/late lane. |
| Taking ego duel after advantage | Throws numbers. | After pick, group and force trades. |

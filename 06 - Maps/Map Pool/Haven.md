# Haven

> Current focus: **Stop rotating on first sound. Confirm spike/contact before leaving anchor side.**

## Map Identity

Three-site rotation map. Winning means manipulating defender positions and not over-rotating on defense.

### Main Win Conditions

- Pressure two sites before committing.
- Use Garage/Mid control to split C or B.
- On defense, anchor longer than your instinct says.


### Important Areas

- A Long / A Short
- Sewer
- Garage
- B Site
- C Long
- C Link


### Rotation Characteristics

- Very fast attacker pivots; defenders can over-rotate and leave sites empty.

### Common Defender Setups

- Common setups: A anchor, B/Mid player, Garage player, C anchor, floating rotator.

### Common Attacker Default

- Default A Long/Sewer + Garage/C pressure, then explode where defender utility is weakest.

### Best Controller From My Pool

- Omen is very strong for flexible global smokes; Astra strong for site control; Clove good for ranked tempo.

## Attack

### Default Responsibilities

| Phase | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Early round | Take safe space, deny info, keep spike protected, avoid first death. |
| Mid-round | Read utility/rotations, call regroup or split, preserve one smoke if possible. |
| Late round | Smoke final choke, trade plant, set post-plant crossfire. |

### Default Plan

- Show A and C presence early to stretch rotations.
- Fight Garage with support; Garage control unlocks B/C split.
- If defenders push extremities, hold and punish before executing.
- Use fake smokes to pull third defender away from target site.

### A Execute

- Take A Long or Sewer first; avoid both players dying before contact.
- Smoke Heaven and CT/Link side.
- Clear close site/short cubby/hell depending on route.
- Plant for Long if Long is controlled; otherwise plant safe.
- Post-plant: one Long, one site/hell, one flank watch if possible.

### B Execute

- B hits work best from Mid/Garage pressure, not dry walk through Mid alone.
- Smoke both side links or isolate one side with utility and fight the other.
- Plant fast and reposition because B retakes collapse quickly.
- Post-plant: play crossfire from Mid/Garage/B box, not all same angle.

### C Execute

- Take C Long with patience; clear close right/left and platform/back site.
- Smoke Garage/CT depending on split.
- Garage split makes C much easier.
- Plant for Long when possible.
- Post-plant: hold Long crossfire and deny Garage flank.

### Mid-Round Rules

- Garage control is the map hinge.
- If defenders give Garage, split C/B repeatedly until they adapt.
- If defenders fight Garage hard, fake it then hit A fast.


### Anti-Eco Rules

- Do not walk alone into close shotgun corners.
- Use one smoke to cross dangerous long angle, then clear close with numbers.
- Keep spike away from the first-contact player.
- If you get first pick, slow down and trade; do not donate weapons.

### Bonus Round Rules

- Avoid dry long-range fights versus rifles.
- Use numbers and utility to isolate one defender.
- If spike is planted, bonus is already successful; play post-plant trades.

## Defense

### Setup 1 - Standard Information Setup

- Default: controller supports A/C from safe rotating position. Chamber can hold C Long or A Long OP angle. Reyna can contest Garage with flash support.

### Setup 2 - Adapt / Retake Setup

- Retake B/C setup: give first contact, keep numbers alive, smoke planted spike routes late.

### Rotation Rules

- Rotate on **spike seen**, **multiple enemies confirmed**, or **key utility committed**, not on one footstep.
- If you are the anchor, your first job is to stay alive and delay.
- If teammate dies alone, do not instantly replace the same angle without utility.
- Call what you are giving up: `I am leaving B Main`, `I am holding Tree`, `I have no smoke for retake`.

### Anchor Rules

- One kill and survival is better than two kills and no delay if the site collapses.
- Use smoke to split the execute wave.
- Fight when attackers cross utility, plant, or turn from teammate contact.

## Retakes

- A retake: smoke Long or Heaven gap depending on plant, clear Hell/Default first.
- B retake: wait for both links, use smoke on one side and swing from two directions.
- C retake: smoke Long, clear close site/logs/platform, then isolate back site.


## Pistol Rounds

### Attack Pistol

- C split or A explode. Reyna Leer is strong in tight site entries.

### Defense Pistol

- Stack 2 Garage/C or 2 A Short/Long. Chamber Headhunter can own C Long if aim is warm.

## Agent Plans

### Omen

- Flexible smokes across three sites are high value.
- Paranoia Garage, A Short, or C Long execute lanes.
- Ult can fake opposite site or retrieve spike from dangerous lane.

### Astra

- Stars on Garage/B/A Short create map-wide pressure.
- Gravity Well for B plant denial or C default retake.
- Recall stars to fake C/A and pull rotates.

### Clove

- Ruse quickly for A/C hits and still fight with team.
- Meddle Garage/B tight fights.
- Ult after entry trade to plant or stall retake.

### Reyna

- Strong Garage/A Short contact agent with Leer.
- Do not solo lurk C every round; Haven needs pressure sync.
- Dismiss out after first Long duel.

### Chamber

- OP C Long/A Long with safe Rendezvous.
- Trademark Garage or flank depending on comp.
- Play anchor until spike confirmed; do not over-rotate early.


## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Loses | Correction |
|---|---|---|
| Smoking instantly every round | Gives defenders timing and lets them wait it out. | Smoke with pressure or for a specific crossing. |
| Dying before second layer utility | Team loses execute/retake power. | Play one step behind first contact unless on Reyna/Chamber plan. |
| Rotating on sound only | Leaves site open to fakes. | Ask for spike, contact count, or utility confirmation. |
| Playing post-plant from one lane | Easy retake collapse. | Create crossfire: main + site + flank/late lane. |
| Taking ego duel after advantage | Throws numbers. | After pick, group and force trades. |

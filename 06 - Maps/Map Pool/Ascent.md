# Ascent

> Current focus: **Win Mid without donating first death. Use smoke timing to convert picks.**

## Map Identity

Classic mid-control map. If Mid is lost for free, both sites become weak and rotations become readable.

### Main Win Conditions

- Fight for Mid without overcommitting bodies.
- Use A Main/B Main pressure to force utility, then re-hit with late smoke.
- On defense, keep Cat/Tree and Market/B Main information alive as long as possible.


### Important Areas

- Mid Top / Tiles / Catwalk
- A Main
- Tree
- B Main
- Market
- Lane/Boat House


### Rotation Characteristics

- Fast rotations through Mid/Market/Tree; lurks punish early rotates hard.

### Common Defender Setups

- Common setups: 1 A anchor, 1 Tree/Cat, 1 Mid, 1 B anchor, 1 flexible rotator.

### Common Attacker Default

- 1 A Main pressure, 1 B Main pressure, 2 Mid control, controller flexible with spike or Mid.

### Best Controller From My Pool

- Omen for ranked simplicity; Astra for set teamplay; Clove for tempo/ranked fighting.

## Attack

### Default Responsibilities

| Phase | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Early round | Take safe space, deny info, keep spike protected, avoid first death. |
| Mid-round | Read utility/rotations, call regroup or split, preserve one smoke if possible. |
| Late round | Smoke final choke, trade plant, set post-plant crossfire. |

### Default Plan

- Smoke Top Mid or Catwalk pressure depending on plan.
- Do not dry swing Mid into OP/Cat without flash/recon/Leer.
- Use B Main noise to pull rotator, then pressure Cat/A or split B.
- If defenders fight Mid every round, punish with delayed re-clear and crossfire.

### A Execute

- Take A Main space first; clear close, Wine, Generator, Dice, Heaven pressure.
- Smoke Heaven + Tree/door area.
- Entry clears close site while controller stays alive for post-plant smoke.
- Plant default only if Heaven/Tree are controlled or smoked.
- Post-plant: do not all sit Main; one player plays site/Gen if safe.

### B Execute

- Take B Main with utility; clear close left, switch, lane pressure.
- Smoke Market + CT/Stairs.
- Entry splits Lane/Boat House while second wave trades.
- Plant safe/default depending on Market control.
- Post-plant: avoid 5 B Main; keep one Lane/Site if numbers allow.

### Mid-Round Rules

- If Mid is free twice, take it faster and split.
- If Mid is contested, bait utility then hit a main lane.
- If Killjoy/Cypher utility is heavy B, pressure A/Tree.
- Never rotate through attacker spawn slowly after giving defenders sound unless you leave a lurk.


### Anti-Eco Rules

- Do not walk alone into close shotgun corners.
- Use one smoke to cross dangerous long angle, then clear close with numbers.
- Keep spike away from the first-contact player.
- If you get first pick, slow down and trade; do not donate weapons.

### Bonus Round Rules

- Avoid dry long-range fights versus rifles.
- Use numbers and utility to isolate one defender.
- If spike is planted, bonus is already successful; play post-plant trades.

## Defense

### Setup 1 - Standard Information Setup

- Omen/Astra/Clove plays Mid/Tree support. One smoke ready for A Main or B Main choke. Chamber can hold B Main/Market timing. Reyna fights only with escape/trade path.

### Setup 2 - Adapt / Retake Setup

- Retake A setup: give A Main, keep Tree/Heaven, smoke Main after plant, fight together from Heaven/Tree/Gen.

### Rotation Rules

- Rotate on **spike seen**, **multiple enemies confirmed**, or **key utility committed**, not on one footstep.
- If you are the anchor, your first job is to stay alive and delay.
- If teammate dies alone, do not instantly replace the same angle without utility.
- Call what you are giving up: `I am leaving B Main`, `I am holding Tree`, `I have no smoke for retake`.

### Anchor Rules

- One kill and survival is better than two kills and no delay if the site collapses.
- Use smoke to split the execute wave.
- Fight when attackers cross utility, plant, or turn from teammate contact.

## Retakes

- A retake: smoke A Main or Dice/Gen gap, clear close site, then Heaven/Tree swing together.
- B retake: smoke B Main or Lane, clear Boat House then default, never trickle through Market alone.
- Save Paranoia/Gravity/Meddle for retake when anchoring weak side.


## Pistol Rounds

### Attack Pistol

- Ghost/Classic armor. Contact A with Tree smoke or B split with Market smoke. Reyna can lead with Leer; Chamber buys Headhunter if confident.

### Defense Pistol

- Fight Mid with two or play retake A. Do not solo wide swing B Main without trade.

## Agent Plans

### Omen

- Smoke Top Mid/Cat or A Heaven/Tree depending on hit.
- Paranoia through A Main/Tree or B Main/Lane to win entry timing.
- TP to off-angle after smoke, not before teammates contact.

### Astra

- Stars: Mid choke, A Tree/Generator, B Lane/Market.
- Pull default plant/defuse spots; stun Tree or Lane for execute.
- Recall Mid star to fake pressure and force rotations.

### Clove

- Ruse Heaven/Tree or Market/CT, then fight with team.
- Meddle close A Main/B Main pockets before swing.
- Ult for spike plant/trade, not solo revenge.

### Reyna

- Leer Mid/Cat or A Main entry; Dismiss after first pick into cover.
- Do not lurk every round; Ascent needs traded Mid pressure.
- Strong on B Main punish if defenders fight early.

### Chamber

- Hold B Main/Market or A Main OP timing with Rendezvous ready.
- Trademark flank Cat/B Main depending on team setup.
- Play retake with Headhunter angles, not body-stack site.


## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Loses | Correction |
|---|---|---|
| Smoking instantly every round | Gives defenders timing and lets them wait it out. | Smoke with pressure or for a specific crossing. |
| Dying before second layer utility | Team loses execute/retake power. | Play one step behind first contact unless on Reyna/Chamber plan. |
| Rotating on sound only | Leaves site open to fakes. | Ask for spike, contact count, or utility confirmation. |
| Playing post-plant from one lane | Easy retake collapse. | Create crossfire: main + site + flank/late lane. |
| Taking ego duel after advantage | Throws numbers. | After pick, group and force trades. |

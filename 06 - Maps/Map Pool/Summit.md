# Summit

> Current focus: **Wall discipline. Only drop a wall when your team gains more than it loses.**

## Map Identity

New three-lane, two-site map built around permanent droppable walls on A, Mid, and B. Wall timing changes the entire round.

### Main Win Conditions

- Control Mid/Fountain area because it links both sites.
- Use droppable walls only after space is secured or to deny a confirmed split.
- Avoid closing your own rotation path before the spike plan is clear.


### Important Areas

- A Lobby / A Main
- A Garden / A Link
- Mid Fountain
- Mid Bend / Mid Bottom
- B Main
- B Link / B Tower


### Rotation Characteristics

- Mid control creates rapid pivots. Dropped walls can permanently cut or reshape rotations for the round.

### Common Defender Setups

- Common concept: one A anchor, one B anchor, one Mid controller/sentinel layer, one rotator, one aggressive info player.

### Common Attacker Default

- Default both mains while two players contest Mid. Decide site only after wall status and defender positions are known.

### Best Controller From My Pool

- Omen for flexible first learning; Astra for coordinated Mid/site control; Clove for aggressive ranked tempo after smokes.

## Attack

### Default Responsibilities

| Phase | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Early round | Take safe space, deny info, keep spike protected, avoid first death. |
| Mid-round | Read utility/rotations, call regroup or split, preserve one smoke if possible. |
| Late round | Smoke final choke, trade plant, set post-plant crossfire. |

### Default Plan

- Learn wall status every round: A wall, Mid wall, B wall.
- Do not trigger a wall just because it exists; ask what path it removes.
- Take Mid Fountain before committing if defenders are rotating well.
- After dropping a post-plant wall, reposition immediately because retake routes are now predictable.

### A Execute

- Take A Main and clear Garden/close site before plant.
- Smoke defender link/CT-style angle and isolate A Link if not cleared.
- Drop A wall only after site space is won or when it blocks a known retake route.
- Plant in a spot your team can actually defend after the wall state changes.
- Post-plant: do not all hide Main; hold the new wall-created choke.

### B Execute

- Take B Main carefully; clear Close Box/Triples-style cover and deeper defender angles.
- Smoke B Link / Tower-side sightline depending on defender pressure.
- Use Mid-to-B split when B Main is stalled.
- Drop B wall after plant to simplify retake only if your own teammates are not locked out.
- Post-plant: assign one player to wall route, one to Main, one to site trade.

### Mid-Round Rules

- Mid wall is a commitment tool, not free stall.
- If you control Mid, keep it open until the hit is called.
- If defenders are splitting through Mid, drop Mid wall after they reveal pressure.
- If wall is dropped against you, instantly reroute instead of staring at it.


### Anti-Eco Rules

- Do not walk alone into close shotgun corners.
- Use one smoke to cross dangerous long angle, then clear close with numbers.
- Keep spike away from the first-contact player.
- If you get first pick, slow down and trade; do not donate weapons.

### Bonus Round Rules

- Avoid dry long-range fights versus rifles.
- Use numbers and utility to isolate one defender.
- If spike is planted, bonus is already successful; play post-plant trades.

## Defense

### Setup 1 - Standard Information Setup

- Default Mid-aware setup: controller plays safe enough to smoke A/B reaction. Chamber watches long lane. Reyna takes only supported Mid/A Main fight.

### Setup 2 - Adapt / Retake Setup

- Wall trap round: allow attackers to take shallow space, then drop wall to isolate first wave and fight with numbers.

### Rotation Rules

- Rotate on **spike seen**, **multiple enemies confirmed**, or **key utility committed**, not on one footstep.
- If you are the anchor, your first job is to stay alive and delay.
- If teammate dies alone, do not instantly replace the same angle without utility.
- Call what you are giving up: `I am leaving B Main`, `I am holding Tree`, `I have no smoke for retake`.

### Anchor Rules

- One kill and survival is better than two kills and no delay if the site collapses.
- Use smoke to split the execute wave.
- Fight when attackers cross utility, plant, or turn from teammate contact.

## Retakes

- Before retake, call wall state first.
- Smoke the strongest post-plant lane, not the prettiest minimap spot.
- If wall blocks standard retake, group for the remaining route instead of trickling.
- Use Meddle/Paranoia/Gravity Well to clear tight wall-created chokes.


## Pistol Rounds

### Attack Pistol

- Default Mid + one main. Use Reyna Leer/Clove Meddle to win first choke, then hit before rotations stabilize.

### Defense Pistol

- Fight for info, not hero kills. If wall is used, use it to isolate attackers already committed.

## Agent Plans

### Omen

- Best learning controller: flexible smokes while you learn wall timings.
- Smoke Mid/A Link/B Link based on wall state.
- Paranoia through wall-created chokes after a wall drops.

### Astra

- Strong if team comms wall plan. Stars control Mid Fountain and site links.
- Pull players trapped by dropped walls.
- Recall stars to fake one side before using wall commitment elsewhere.

### Clove

- Good ranked tempo. Smoke first, then fight Mid or main.
- Meddle tight wall chokes and close boxes before contact.
- Ult can convert wall-isolated chaos, but do not revive into a closed path.

### Reyna

- Leer for Mid Fountain and tight site entries.
- Dismiss after first pick before walls isolate you.
- Do not chase through wall routes alone; Summit punishes separation.

### Chamber

- Hold long main/Mid lane with Rendezvous prepped.
- Trademark flank or wall-bypassing timing.
- OP value depends on wall state; reposition instantly when wall drops.


## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Loses | Correction |
|---|---|---|
| Smoking instantly every round | Gives defenders timing and lets them wait it out. | Smoke with pressure or for a specific crossing. |
| Dying before second layer utility | Team loses execute/retake power. | Play one step behind first contact unless on Reyna/Chamber plan. |
| Rotating on sound only | Leaves site open to fakes. | Ask for spike, contact count, or utility confirmation. |
| Playing post-plant from one lane | Easy retake collapse. | Create crossfire: main + site + flank/late lane. |
| Taking ego duel after advantage | Throws numbers. | After pick, group and force trades. |

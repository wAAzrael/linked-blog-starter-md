# Lotus

> Current focus: **Use doors deliberately. Do not autopilot five-man A every attack half.**

## Map Identity

Three-site pressure map with doors and fast pivots. Teams win by owning space before the hit, not by five-man gambling.

### Main Win Conditions

- Fight A Main/Rubble control early.
- Use door timings to create fake pressure.
- Regroup fast after forcing sentinel/controller utility.


### Important Areas

- A Main / Rubble
- A Tree / Door
- B Main
- C Main / Mound
- C Waterfall
- Defender Spawn links


### Rotation Characteristics

- Attackers can pivot quickly through doors and links; defenders must keep info or they rotate blind.

### Common Defender Setups

- Common setups: A contest, B lean/rotator, C anchor, door info/trap.

### Common Attacker Default

- Pressure A Main/Rubble and C Main, then decide between A split, C hit, or late B pinch.

### Best Controller From My Pool

- Omen/Astra both strong. Clove is good when you plan to fight space after smoking.

## Attack

### Default Responsibilities

| Phase | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Early round | Take safe space, deny info, keep spike protected, avoid first death. |
| Mid-round | Read utility/rotations, call regroup or split, preserve one smoke if possible. |
| Late round | Smoke final choke, trade plant, set post-plant crossfire. |

### Default Plan

- Fight for A Main with teammate utility; do not solo ego peek Rubble.
- Break/pressure doors to force defenders to watch two timings.
- C Main control is valuable even if ending A.
- B is strongest as a late punish, not a brainless 5-man.

### A Execute

- Take A Main/Rubble; clear close and tree pressure.
- Smoke Heaven/Drop and Tree/Link depending on split.
- Use door or Tree split to prevent all defenders fighting from one lane.
- Plant safe unless Tree is controlled.
- Post-plant: play Main + Site + Tree if numbers allow.

### B Execute

- B is small: smoke both side links or isolate one and fight the other.
- Use B hit after A/C pressure pulls defenders wide.
- Plant fast, then reposition to crossfires.
- Do not stay clumped in B Main after plant.

### Mid-Round Rules

- Door usage is timing utility. Break it to fake, open it to split, ignore it to punish rotations.
- If defenders overfight A, late C/B becomes free.
- If defenders stack C, A split is the punish.


### Anti-Eco Rules

- Do not walk alone into close shotgun corners.
- Use one smoke to cross dangerous long angle, then clear close with numbers.
- Keep spike away from the first-contact player.
- If you get first pick, slow down and trade; do not donate weapons.

### Bonus Round Rules

- Avoid dry long-range fights versus rifles.
- Use numbers and utility to isolate one defender.
- If spike is planted, bonus is already successful; play post-plant trades.

## Defense

### Setup 1 - Standard Information Setup

- Contest A with support, then fall. Controller smokes A Main or C Main after contact. Chamber can hold C Main/A Main first pick.

### Setup 2 - Adapt / Retake Setup

- Retake-heavy B/C setup: stay alive, use smoke to isolate planted spike, pinch from links.

### Rotation Rules

- Rotate on **spike seen**, **multiple enemies confirmed**, or **key utility committed**, not on one footstep.
- If you are the anchor, your first job is to stay alive and delay.
- If teammate dies alone, do not instantly replace the same angle without utility.
- Call what you are giving up: `I am leaving B Main`, `I am holding Tree`, `I have no smoke for retake`.

### Anchor Rules

- One kill and survival is better than two kills and no delay if the site collapses.
- Use smoke to split the execute wave.
- Fight when attackers cross utility, plant, or turn from teammate contact.

## Retakes

- A retake: clear site/close, smoke Main or Tree depending on where attackers are.
- B retake: wait for two links, use decay/pull/paranoia to clear small site.
- C retake: smoke Main, clear close corners, fight together from waterfall/link.


## Pistol Rounds

### Attack Pistol

- A split or C hit. Reyna can take first contact with Leer but must be traded.

### Defense Pistol

- Fight A early with two or play C crossfire. Avoid solo B death.

## Agent Plans

### Omen

- Smoke A Tree/Heaven or C Link/Waterfall depending on hit.
- Paranoia through A Main/Tree or C Main choke is fight-winning.
- TP into off-angle after door pressure can punish rotations.

### Astra

- Stars on A Main, Tree, C Main, B choke.
- Pull/stun around doors to punish rotations.
- Recall A/C stars to sell fakes and hit B late.

### Clove

- Aggressive Ruse + Meddle for A Main/C Main fights.
- Strong ranked tempo when doors create chaos.
- Ult to keep site hit alive after first trade.

### Reyna

- Leer through A Main/Rubble or C Main to take first fight.
- Great for punishing door swings.
- Avoid selfish lurks; Lotus needs regroup speed.

### Chamber

- Hold C Main or A Main pick with Rendezvous.
- Trademark flank/door timing.
- Good on defense when playing deep retake angles.


## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Loses | Correction |
|---|---|---|
| Smoking instantly every round | Gives defenders timing and lets them wait it out. | Smoke with pressure or for a specific crossing. |
| Dying before second layer utility | Team loses execute/retake power. | Play one step behind first contact unless on Reyna/Chamber plan. |
| Rotating on sound only | Leaves site open to fakes. | Ask for spike, contact count, or utility confirmation. |
| Playing post-plant from one lane | Easy retake collapse. | Create crossfire: main + site + flank/late lane. |
| Taking ego duel after advantage | Throws numbers. | After pick, group and force trades. |

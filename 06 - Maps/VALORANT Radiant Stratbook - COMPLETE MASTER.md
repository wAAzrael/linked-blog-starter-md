# Controller Radiant System

## Identity
Your job is not just to smoke. Your job is to decide which fights are allowed to happen.

As a controller/flex player, your round has four jobs:

1. **Enable the first contact**: make your duelist/Reyna/entry fight one angle instead of three.
2. **Deny information**: force defenders to guess whether pressure is real, fake, or delayed.
3. **Preserve round-winning utility**: never spend both smokes early unless the round is meant to explode.
4. **Win the second plan**: after the first default breaks, your next smoke decides whether the team hits, rotates, lurks, or saves.

## Smoke Timing Rules

| Situation | Smoke Timing | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Fast execute | Smoke 1-2 seconds before entry contact | Your team must cross while smoke is fresh. |
| Default | Hold at least one smoke until info is gained | Early smokes without pressure give free timing to defenders. |
| Fake | Smoke + sound, then stop | Make defenders rotate before your team commits. |
| Retake | Smoke deep, not shallow | Shallow retake smokes give attackers free close fights. |
| Post-plant | Smoke the defuser angle or main retake lane | Delay without giving them a free stick. |

## Ranked Controller Rule
If your team is silent, call the simplest version:

> "Default first. Don't die early. I smoke when we contact. If we get a pick, we group and hit."

This single call stops most Immortal ranked disasters.

## Utility Economy

- **Omen**: one smoke for early control, one smoke for execute/retake. Paranoia should win a lane, not just annoy.
- **Astra**: place stars for threat, not decoration. Recall stars to fake pressure and keep late-round power.
- **Clove**: smoke for tempo. You can take more fights, but you still cannot die before using your first impact smoke.
- **Reyna**: Leer should create a timing, not replace clearing. Your Dismiss is for escaping traded space, not ego-swinging into five.
- **Chamber**: play for one clean kill and survival. If Rendezvous is down, you are no longer allowed to hold like an Operator turret.

## Mid-Round Decision Tree

- **We got first pick** → group, deny info, avoid solo peeks, convert space.
- **We lost first pick** → slow down, default again, hunt isolated defender, avoid dry execute.
- **Enemy used 2+ key utilities** → re-hit that lane after cooldown/rotation window.
- **Enemy stacked site** → fake pressure, rotate, leave lurk late.
- **We have spike planted** → stop hunting unless you have number advantage and teammate trade spacing.

## What To Review After Every Loss

| Question | Yes/No | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Did I die before using core utility? |  | Play one layer safer early. |
| Did I smoke too early with no pressure? |  | Delay smokes until contact/info. |
| Did I rotate without confirming spike/contact? |  | Anchor longer or ask for info. |
| Did I retake without waiting for teammates? |  | Count bodies before swinging. |
| Did I take a fight that my utility could have made easier? |  | Utility first, ego second. |

# Ascent

> Current focus: **Win Mid without donating first death. Use smoke timing to convert picks.**

## Map Identity

Classic mid-control map. If Mid is lost for free, both sites become weak and rotations become readable.

### Main Win Conditions

- Fight for Mid without overcommitting bodies.
- Use A Main/B Main pressure to force utility, then re-hit with late smoke.
- On defense, keep Cat/Tree and Market/B Main information alive as long as possible.


### Important Areas

- Mid Top / Tiles / Catwalk
- A Main
- Tree
- B Main
- Market
- Lane/Boat House


### Rotation Characteristics

- Fast rotations through Mid/Market/Tree; lurks punish early rotates hard.

### Common Defender Setups

- Common setups: 1 A anchor, 1 Tree/Cat, 1 Mid, 1 B anchor, 1 flexible rotator.

### Common Attacker Default

- 1 A Main pressure, 1 B Main pressure, 2 Mid control, controller flexible with spike or Mid.

### Best Controller From My Pool

- Omen for ranked simplicity; Astra for set teamplay; Clove for tempo/ranked fighting.

## Attack

### Default Responsibilities

| Phase | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Early round | Take safe space, deny info, keep spike protected, avoid first death. |
| Mid-round | Read utility/rotations, call regroup or split, preserve one smoke if possible. |
| Late round | Smoke final choke, trade plant, set post-plant crossfire. |

### Default Plan

- Smoke Top Mid or Catwalk pressure depending on plan.
- Do not dry swing Mid into OP/Cat without flash/recon/Leer.
- Use B Main noise to pull rotator, then pressure Cat/A or split B.
- If defenders fight Mid every round, punish with delayed re-clear and crossfire.

### A Execute

- Take A Main space first; clear close, Wine, Generator, Dice, Heaven pressure.
- Smoke Heaven + Tree/door area.
- Entry clears close site while controller stays alive for post-plant smoke.
- Plant default only if Heaven/Tree are controlled or smoked.
- Post-plant: do not all sit Main; one player plays site/Gen if safe.

### B Execute

- Take B Main with utility; clear close left, switch, lane pressure.
- Smoke Market + CT/Stairs.
- Entry splits Lane/Boat House while second wave trades.
- Plant safe/default depending on Market control.
- Post-plant: avoid 5 B Main; keep one Lane/Site if numbers allow.

### Mid-Round Rules

- If Mid is free twice, take it faster and split.
- If Mid is contested, bait utility then hit a main lane.
- If Killjoy/Cypher utility is heavy B, pressure A/Tree.
- Never rotate through attacker spawn slowly after giving defenders sound unless you leave a lurk.


### Anti-Eco Rules

- Do not walk alone into close shotgun corners.
- Use one smoke to cross dangerous long angle, then clear close with numbers.
- Keep spike away from the first-contact player.
- If you get first pick, slow down and trade; do not donate weapons.

### Bonus Round Rules

- Avoid dry long-range fights versus rifles.
- Use numbers and utility to isolate one defender.
- If spike is planted, bonus is already successful; play post-plant trades.

## Defense

### Setup 1 - Standard Information Setup

- Omen/Astra/Clove plays Mid/Tree support. One smoke ready for A Main or B Main choke. Chamber can hold B Main/Market timing. Reyna fights only with escape/trade path.

### Setup 2 - Adapt / Retake Setup

- Retake A setup: give A Main, keep Tree/Heaven, smoke Main after plant, fight together from Heaven/Tree/Gen.

### Rotation Rules

- Rotate on **spike seen**, **multiple enemies confirmed**, or **key utility committed**, not on one footstep.
- If you are the anchor, your first job is to stay alive and delay.
- If teammate dies alone, do not instantly replace the same angle without utility.
- Call what you are giving up: `I am leaving B Main`, `I am holding Tree`, `I have no smoke for retake`.

### Anchor Rules

- One kill and survival is better than two kills and no delay if the site collapses.
- Use smoke to split the execute wave.
- Fight when attackers cross utility, plant, or turn from teammate contact.

## Retakes

- A retake: smoke A Main or Dice/Gen gap, clear close site, then Heaven/Tree swing together.
- B retake: smoke B Main or Lane, clear Boat House then default, never trickle through Market alone.
- Save Paranoia/Gravity/Meddle for retake when anchoring weak side.


## Pistol Rounds

### Attack Pistol

- Ghost/Classic armor. Contact A with Tree smoke or B split with Market smoke. Reyna can lead with Leer; Chamber buys Headhunter if confident.

### Defense Pistol

- Fight Mid with two or play retake A. Do not solo wide swing B Main without trade.

## Agent Plans

### Omen

- Smoke Top Mid/Cat or A Heaven/Tree depending on hit.
- Paranoia through A Main/Tree or B Main/Lane to win entry timing.
- TP to off-angle after smoke, not before teammates contact.

### Astra

- Stars: Mid choke, A Tree/Generator, B Lane/Market.
- Pull default plant/defuse spots; stun Tree or Lane for execute.
- Recall Mid star to fake pressure and force rotations.

### Clove

- Ruse Heaven/Tree or Market/CT, then fight with team.
- Meddle close A Main/B Main pockets before swing.
- Ult for spike plant/trade, not solo revenge.

### Reyna

- Leer Mid/Cat or A Main entry; Dismiss after first pick into cover.
- Do not lurk every round; Ascent needs traded Mid pressure.
- Strong on B Main punish if defenders fight early.

### Chamber

- Hold B Main/Market or A Main OP timing with Rendezvous ready.
- Trademark flank Cat/B Main depending on team setup.
- Play retake with Headhunter angles, not body-stack site.


## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Loses | Correction |
|---|---|---|
| Smoking instantly every round | Gives defenders timing and lets them wait it out. | Smoke with pressure or for a specific crossing. |
| Dying before second layer utility | Team loses execute/retake power. | Play one step behind first contact unless on Reyna/Chamber plan. |
| Rotating on sound only | Leaves site open to fakes. | Ask for spike, contact count, or utility confirmation. |
| Playing post-plant from one lane | Easy retake collapse. | Create crossfire: main + site + flank/late lane. |
| Taking ego duel after advantage | Throws numbers. | After pick, group and force trades. |

# Breeze

> Current focus: **Long-range calm. Cross open space only with smoke, flash, or teammate trade.**

## Map Identity

Long-range, space-heavy map. Poor spacing and panic rotates lose more rounds than bad aim.

### Main Win Conditions

- Control long lanes with patience, not random wide peeks.
- Use late smokes to cross open space.
- Keep flank control because rotations are long and lurks are round-winning.


### Important Areas

- A Main / Cave
- Mid Pillar / Mid Nest
- B Main
- Elbow
- Tunnel
- Halls


### Rotation Characteristics

- Long rotations. Once spike is seen, defenders often over-rotate slowly; attackers can punish with late lurks.

### Common Defender Setups

- Common setups: anchors deep, one Mid info player, one Halls/rotate player.

### Common Attacker Default

- Spread map, deny OP info, take Mid/Halls slowly, then group for site hit.

Three-site rotation map. Winning means manipulating defender positions and not over-rotating on defense.

### Main Win Conditions

- Pressure two sites before committing.
- Use Garage/Mid control to split C or B.
- On defense, anchor longer than your instinct says.


### Important Areas

- A Long / A Short
- Sewer
- Garage
- B Site
- C Long
- C Link

- Very fast attacker pivots; defenders can over-rotate and leave sites empty.

> Current focus: **Layer utility before Mid fights. Dry Mid deaths ruin Split halves.**

## Map Identity

Compressed vertical map. Mid/Ropes control decides both sites, and utility layering is stronger than raw dry peeks.

### Main Win Conditions

- Win Mid/Ropes pressure or convincingly fake it.
- Use smokes to cross tight chokes.
- On defense, deny Mid without dying alone.


### Important Areas

- A Main
- A Ramps
- Ropes
- Mid Mail/Vents
- B Main
- B Heaven


### Rotation Characteristics

- Defender rotations are fast through Heaven/Ropes. Attackers need Mid control to stop stacked sites.

### Common Defender Setups

- Common setups: one A anchor, one Ramps, one Mid, one B Heaven, one B anchor/rotator.

### Common Attacker Default

- Pressure Mid with two, hold B Main/A Main pushes, then split A or B through Heaven/Ropes.

### Best Controller From My Pool

- Omen and Astra are excellent. Clove is strong for ranked if you fight after smoking. Reyna thrives in tight Leer fights.

## Attack

### Default Responsibilities

| Phase | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Early round | Take safe space, deny info, keep spike protected, avoid first death. |
| Mid-round | Read utility/rotations, call regroup or split, preserve one smoke if possible. |
| Late round | Smoke final choke, trade plant, set post-plant crossfire. |

### Default Plan

- Mid control is priority; do not dry walk into Mail/Vents crossfire.
- Use smoke on Mail/Vents to isolate one fight.
- Hold defensive pushes from A/B Main while Mid group works.
- If Mid fails twice, fake Mid and fast hit an extremity.

### A Execute

- Take A Main + Ramps if possible.
- Smoke Heaven/Rafters and Screens/CT depending on route.
- Clear close site, Hell, Rafters drop.
- Plant safe if Heaven not controlled; plant for Main if space is won.
- Post-plant: one Main, one site, one Heaven/Ramps if possible.

### B Execute

- Take B Main with utility; clear close, default, back site.
- Smoke Heaven and CT/Alley.
- Best version is B split from Mid/Vents + B Main.
- Post-plant: avoid all B Main; keep one site/backsite if safe.

### Mid-Round Rules

- If defenders smoke Mid early, wait and re-hit with second wave.
- If they fight Mid, punish with stun/flash/Leer and trade.
- If they fully give Mid, split every round until they fight.


### Anti-Eco Rules

- Do not walk alone into close shotgun corners.
- Use one smoke to cross dangerous long angle, then clear close with numbers.
- Keep spike away from the first-contact player.
- If you get first pick, slow down and trade; do not donate weapons.

### Bonus Round Rules

- Avoid dry long-range fights versus rifles.
- Use numbers and utility to isolate one defender.
- If spike is planted, bonus is already successful; play post-plant trades.

## Defense

### Setup 1 - Standard Information Setup

- Controller plays Mid/Ropes support. Smoke Mid choke after contact, not instantly every round. Chamber can hold B Main or A Ramp angle.

### Setup 2 - Adapt / Retake Setup

- Aggressive Ramps/Mid round with Reyna + controller support, then fall after one kill.

### Rotation Rules

- Rotate on **spike seen**, **multiple enemies confirmed**, or **key utility committed**, not on one footstep.
- If you are the anchor, your first job is to stay alive and delay.
- If teammate dies alone, do not instantly replace the same angle without utility.
- Call what you are giving up: `I am leaving B Main`, `I am holding Tree`, `I have no smoke for retake`.

### Anchor Rules

- One kill and survival is better than two kills and no delay if the site collapses.
- Use smoke to split the execute wave.
- Fight when attackers cross utility, plant, or turn from teammate contact.

## Retakes

- A retake: smoke Main or Screens, clear Hell, swing Rafters/site together.
- B retake: smoke B Main, clear close/default/backsite, avoid single-file Heaven drops.
- Save paranoia/decay/pull for clearing tight pockets.


## Pistol Rounds

### Attack Pistol

- B split or A Ramps split. Tight Leer/Classic armor rounds are strong.

### Defense Pistol

- Stack Mid/Ropes or B Heaven. Chamber Headhunter can lock a choke.

## Agent Plans

### Omen

- Premium pick. Smoke Mail/Vents, Heaven, CT.
- Paranoia through Mid/Ropes/A Main wins compressed fights.
- TP to boxes/one-and-done angles after smokes.

### Astra

- Excellent for Mid control. Pull Mail/Vents or B default.
- Stun Ropes/Rafters for site hits.
- Recall Mid star to fake pressure and split late.

### Clove

- Strong for taking Mid fights after Ruse.
- Meddle tight corners before Reyna/you swing.
- Ult has high value because sites are close and trades happen fast.

### Reyna

- Very strong on Split: Leer through tight chokes and Dismiss out.
- Fight Mid/Ramps with support, not solo.
- Avoid overheal greed after first kill.

### Chamber

- Hold B Main/A Ramp or Mid angle with Rendezvous.
- Trademark flank or Ropes depending on team.
- Headhunter strong in pistol/eco tight angles.


## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Loses | Correction |
|---|---|---|
| Smoking instantly every round | Gives defenders timing and lets them wait it out. | Smoke with pressure or for a specific crossing. |
| Dying before second layer utility | Team loses execute/retake power. | Play one step behind first contact unless on Reyna/Chamber plan. |

# Bind

> Current focus: **Use teleporters with purpose. Loud rotate is a strat, not a panic button.**

## Map Identity

No-mid map with teleporters. Rounds are won by extremity control, fake pressure, and fast regrouping.

### Main Win Conditions

- Control Showers/Hookah without losing spike.
- Use teleporter rotations as pressure, not panic.
- Punish defenders who over-stack one site.


### Important Areas

- A Short
- Showers
- U-Hall/Lamps
- Hookah
- B Long
- Elbow
- Teleporters


### Rotation Characteristics

- No middle means rotations are loud but fast through teleporters. Fakes are powerful if you keep utility.

### Common Defender Setups

- Common setups: A Short/Lamps, Showers contest, Hookah, B Long, rotator.

### Common Attacker Default

- Pressure A Short/Showers and B Long/Hookah, then collapse on weakest anchor.

### Best Controller From My Pool

- Clove/Omen strong for ranked; Astra strong for set executes.

## Attack

### Default Responsibilities

| Phase | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Early round | Take safe space, deny info, keep spike protected, avoid first death. |
| Mid-round | Read utility/rotations, call regroup or split, preserve one smoke if possible. |
| Late round | Smoke final choke, trade plant, set post-plant crossfire. |

### Default Plan

- Default with spike safe; do not lose spike Showers/Hookah alone.
- Use teleport only after forcing rotations or with a clear fake.
- Break sentinel utility before committing.
- Late lurks through Short/Long punish panic rotates.

### A Execute

- Take A Short and/or Showers.
- Smoke Heaven/Truck-side and Lamps/CT depending on route.
- Clear Lamps/U-Hall before planting if possible.
- Plant for Short/Showers based on space.
- Post-plant: hold Lamps control or hard deny it.

### B Execute

- Take B Long and Hookah pinch if possible.
- Smoke Elbow and CT/Back site angle.
- Clear close Hookah/drop and Cubby/Tube-style angles.
- Plant for Long if Long is held.
- Post-plant: avoid all Hookah; Long crossfire is king.

### Mid-Round Rules

- There is no Mid: your mid-round is teleporter/fake discipline.
- If defenders stack B, fake B sound and walk A.
- If defenders push extremities, hold and punish.


### Anti-Eco Rules

- Do not walk alone into close shotgun corners.
- Use one smoke to cross dangerous long angle, then clear close with numbers.
- Keep spike away from the first-contact player.
- If you get first pick, slow down and trade; do not donate weapons.

### Bonus Round Rules

- Avoid dry long-range fights versus rifles.
- Use numbers and utility to isolate one defender.
- If spike is planted, bonus is already successful; play post-plant trades.

## Defense

### Setup 1 - Standard Information Setup

- Contest one extremity with two, play safe anchor on the other. Chamber can OP B Long/Showers then escape.

### Setup 2 - Adapt / Retake Setup

- Retake setup: give site if needed, keep Lamps/Elbow utility for retake.

### Rotation Rules

- Rotate on **spike seen**, **multiple enemies confirmed**, or **key utility committed**, not on one footstep.
- If you are the anchor, your first job is to stay alive and delay.
- If teammate dies alone, do not instantly replace the same angle without utility.
- Call what you are giving up: `I am leaving B Main`, `I am holding Tree`, `I have no smoke for retake`.

### Anchor Rules

- One kill and survival is better than two kills and no delay if the site collapses.
- Use smoke to split the execute wave.
- Fight when attackers cross utility, plant, or turn from teammate contact.

## Retakes

- A retake: smoke Short or Showers, clear Lamps first.
- B retake: smoke Long or Hookah, clear site from CT/Elbow together.
- Teleport retakes require timing; do not arrive one by one.


## Pistol Rounds

### Attack Pistol

- A Short burst or B Long/Hookah split. Reyna Leer strong in Lamps/Hookah.

### Defense Pistol

- Stack an extremity and fight close. Chamber Headhunter valuable on Long/Showers.

## Agent Plans

### Omen

- Smoke Heaven/Lamps/Elbow/CT. Paranoia through Hookah or Lamps.
- TP can cross Lamps/Hookah after utility but do not gamble spike.
- Ult for fake teleporter pressure or spike retrieval.

### Astra

- Stars Hookah, Lamps, Showers, B Long.
- Pull Lamps/Hookah drop/default plants.
- Recall stars to fake site then teleport rotate.

### Clove

- Great for fast A Short/B Long ranked hits.
- Meddle Lamps/Hookah/Elbow close fights.
- Ult can keep execute alive after entry trade.

### Reyna

- Leer Hookah/Lamps/Showers. Strong first contact if traded.
- Do not lurk with spike in Showers.
- Dismiss back after Long/Short pick.

### Chamber

- OP B Long/Showers. Trademark flank/teleporter timing.
- Rendezvous escape before fighting.


## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Loses | Correction |
|---|---|---|
| Smoking instantly every round | Gives defenders timing and lets them wait it out. | Smoke with pressure or for a specific crossing. |
| Dying before second layer utility | Team loses execute/retake power. | Play one step behind first contact unless on Reyna/Chamber plan. |
| Rotating on sound only | Leaves site open to fakes. | Ask for spike, contact count, or utility confirmation. |
| Playing post-plant from one lane | Easy retake collapse. | Create crossfire: main + site + flank/late lane. |
| Taking ego duel after advantage | Throws numbers. | After pick, group and force trades. |

# Fracture

> Current focus: **Respect pinch timing. One-lane hits are the bronze version of Fracture.**

## Map Identity

Pinch map. Attackers start on both sides, so defenders must choose between fighting early space or playing layered retake.

### Main Win Conditions

- Attack from two sides, not one predictable choke.
- On defense, contest early or hold crossfires; half-measures lose.
- Track flank timings constantly.


### Important Areas

- A Main
- A Dish
- A Drop
- B Main
- B Arcade
- B Tower
- Defender Spawn links


### Rotation Characteristics

- Very fast pressure from opposite sides. Rotations can be cut if defenders lose map control.

### Common Defender Setups

- Common setups: one A anchor, one Dish/Drop, one B anchor, one Arcade/Tower, one flex.

### Common Attacker Default

- Split pressure A Main/Dish or B Main/Arcade. Force defenders to turn around during execute.

### Best Controller From My Pool

- Astra/Omen for coordinated pinches; Clove for aggressive ranked fights.

## Attack

### Default Responsibilities

| Phase | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Early round | Take safe space, deny info, keep spike protected, avoid first death. |
| Mid-round | Read utility/rotations, call regroup or split, preserve one smoke if possible. |
| Late round | Smoke final choke, trade plant, set post-plant crossfire. |

### Default Plan

- Always threaten both sides before commit unless rushing by design.
- Use utility to split defenders’ crosshair placement.
- Leave lurk/flank watch because pushes are common.
- After plant, control one side deeply instead of hiding five on site.

### A Execute

- Pressure A Main and Dish/Drop together.
- Smoke CT/Link and isolate site defenders.
- Clear close site, Drop, Sands-style pockets.
- Plant for Main/Dish depending on control.
- Post-plant: hold crossfire from both sides.

### B Execute

- Pressure B Main and Arcade.
- Smoke Tower/CT and cut spawn rotate.
- Clear close canteen/generator-style positions.
- Plant safe then spread to Arcade/Main.
- Post-plant: deny Tower retake early.

### Mid-Round Rules

- Fracture mid-round equals pinch timing.
- If one side loses space, stop and regroup instead of solo exploding.
- If defenders push one side, collapse opposite quickly.


### Anti-Eco Rules

- Do not walk alone into close shotgun corners.
- Use one smoke to cross dangerous long angle, then clear close with numbers.
- Keep spike away from the first-contact player.
- If you get first pick, slow down and trade; do not donate weapons.

### Bonus Round Rules

- Avoid dry long-range fights versus rifles.
- Use numbers and utility to isolate one defender.
- If spike is planted, bonus is already successful; play post-plant trades.

## Defense

### Setup 1 - Standard Information Setup

- Early fight one lane with two, fall after contact. Chamber can anchor/pick B Main or Dish.

### Setup 2 - Adapt / Retake Setup

- Full retake: give shallow site, save smokes/pull/paranoia for plant denial/retake.

### Rotation Rules

- Rotate on **spike seen**, **multiple enemies confirmed**, or **key utility committed**, not on one footstep.
- If you are the anchor, your first job is to stay alive and delay.
- If teammate dies alone, do not instantly replace the same angle without utility.
- Call what you are giving up: `I am leaving B Main`, `I am holding Tree`, `I have no smoke for retake`.

### Anchor Rules

- One kill and survival is better than two kills and no delay if the site collapses.
- Use smoke to split the execute wave.
- Fight when attackers cross utility, plant, or turn from teammate contact.

## Retakes

- A retake: pinch from spawn + Drop/Dish if possible.
- B retake: Tower control is huge; smoke Main/Arcade based on plant.
- Never retake through same choke with all players.


## Pistol Rounds

### Attack Pistol

- Split A or B instantly; pistol rounds reward fast pinches.

### Defense Pistol

- Stack one side with crossfire and trap first contact.

## Agent Plans

### Omen

- Smoke Tower/CT/Link for pinch hits.
- Paranoia through Arcade/A Main/Drop timing.
- TP can reposition across chaotic site fights.

### Astra

- Excellent for set pinches: stars both sides before collapse.
- Pull plant/default, stun Tower/Drop.
- Recall one side to fake, collapse other.

### Clove

- Strong in ranked chaos if you smoke before fighting.
- Meddle split-site pockets.
- Ult for trade-conversion during fast pinches.

### Reyna

- Leer one side as other side contacts; avoid solo one-lane hit.
- Dismiss back after first pick to keep pinch alive.
- Great for Arcade/Dish fights if supported.

### Chamber

- Hold Dish/B Main/Arcade with TP.
- Trademark flank lane opposite your stack.
- OP can punish predictable attackers but rotate early.


## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Loses | Correction |
|---|---|---|
| Smoking instantly every round | Gives defenders timing and lets them wait it out. | Smoke with pressure or for a specific crossing. |
| Dying before second layer utility | Team loses execute/retake power. | Play one step behind first contact unless on Reyna/Chamber plan. |
| Rotating on sound only | Leaves site open to fakes. | Ask for spike, contact count, or utility confirmation. |
| Playing post-plant from one lane | Easy retake collapse. | Create crossfire: main + site + flank/late lane. |
| Taking ego duel after advantage | Throws numbers. | After pick, group and force trades. |

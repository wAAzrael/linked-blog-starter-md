# Reyna Playbook

## Role Identity

First contact / punish pick / ranked carry when aim is sharp

## Core Rules

- Leer creates unfair fights, not safe full-clears.
- Take first contact only where teammates can trade or you can Dismiss.
- Dismiss after value; Devour only when the round slows and you are safe.
- Your lurk must have timing. A late lurk with no pressure is just baiting.

## Common Bad Habits To Remove

- Dry wide swinging OP angles.
- Throwing Leer too high/late for teammates to use.
- Devouring in the open.
- Chasing overheal fights while spike/space is lost.

## Round Checklist

### Before Barrier Drops

- What space am I responsible for?
- What is my first utility timing?
- Am I allowed to die first this round?
- What is my escape/trade route?

### After First Contact

- Did we get pick or lose pick?
- Is my next job to group, lurk, smoke, anchor, or save?
- Do I have spike responsibility?
- Can I win the round by staying alive instead of fighting?

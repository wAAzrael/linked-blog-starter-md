# Clove Playbook

## Role Identity

Tempo controller / ranked fighter / second-life clutch agent

## Core Rules

- Smoke before taking the space fight, not after dying.
- Meddle is for clearing close pockets and weakening swing timings.
- Pick-Me-Up rewards clean fights but should not justify bad peeks.
- Not Dead Yet is for round conversion: plant, trade, isolate, or delay.

## Common Bad Habits To Remove

- Playing like Reyna and forgetting smoke duty.
- Dying before first smoke on attack.
- Using ult just to run into the same angle again.
- Overfighting after Pick-Me-Up instead of resetting.

## Round Checklist

### Before Barrier Drops

- What space am I responsible for?
- What is my first utility timing?
- Am I allowed to die first this round?
- What is my escape/trade route?

### After First Contact

- Did we get pick or lose pick?
- Is my next job to group, lurk, smoke, anchor, or save?
- Do I have spike responsibility?
- Can I win the round by staying alive instead of fighting?

# Astra Playbook

## Role Identity

Set controller / global map control / utility combo anchor

## Core Rules

- Stars are threats. A placed star should change how enemies move.
- Recall to fake; do not leave dead stars forever.
- Gravity Well wins plant denial, choke punish, and retake clears.
- Nova Pulse is strongest when paired with teammate swing or known corner.

## Common Bad Habits To Remove

- Placing all stars before you know the round plan.
- Using pull/stun with no teammate ready to fight.
- Playing too far from the timing and becoming spectator-controller.
- Saving stars until round is already lost.

## Round Checklist

### Before Barrier Drops

- What space am I responsible for?
- What is my first utility timing?
- Am I allowed to die first this round?
- What is my escape/trade route?

### After First Contact

- Did we get pick or lose pick?
- Is my next job to group, lurk, smoke, anchor, or save?
- Do I have spike responsibility?
- Can I win the round by staying alive instead of fighting?

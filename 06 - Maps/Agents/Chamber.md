# Chamber Playbook

## Role Identity

Sentinel pick agent / Operator anchor / flank control

## Core Rules

- Trademark protects the timing your team cannot watch.
- Rendezvous must be placed before the fight, not after panic.
- Hold one high-value angle, take one shot, leave or reposition.
- Headhunter is an eco equalizer: clean first bullet, no spam panic.

## Common Bad Habits To Remove

- Holding after Rendezvous is gone.
- Putting Trademark where it gives info too late.
- Over-saving Operator instead of taking round-winning space.
- Playing sentinel but never watching flank.

## Round Checklist

### Before Barrier Drops

- What space am I responsible for?
- What is my first utility timing?
- Am I allowed to die first this round?
- What is my escape/trade route?

### After First Contact

- Did we get pick or lose pick?
- Is my next job to group, lurk, smoke, anchor, or save?
- Do I have spike responsibility?
- Can I win the round by staying alive instead of fighting?

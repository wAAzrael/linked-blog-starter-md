# Omen Playbook

## Role Identity

Main controller / lurk-flex / clutch utility

## Core Rules

- First smoke enables space; second smoke wins the mid-round.
- Paranoia must be paired with a swing or entry timing.
- Teleport is for off-angle, escape, reposition, or fake pressure — not random highlight attempts.
- Ult is strongest for spike retrieval, info, fake rotate, or late lurk pressure.

## Common Bad Habits To Remove

- Smoking too early with no team pressure.
- Using Paranoia after teammates already died.
- Teleporting into uncleared space without sound cover.
- Dying first as solo controller.

## Round Checklist

### Before Barrier Drops

- What space am I responsible for?
- What is my first utility timing?
- Am I allowed to die first this round?
- What is my escape/trade route?

### After First Contact

- Did we get pick or lose pick?
- Is my next job to group, lurk, smoke, anchor, or save?
- Do I have spike responsibility?
- Can I win the round by staying alive instead of fighting?
